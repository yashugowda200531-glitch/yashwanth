⚠️ Mula Nakshatra — Important Consideration

Mula nakshatra (ruled by Ketu, deity Nirriti) traditionally carries what is called Mula Dosha — a belief that a child born in Mula, especially in the 1st pada, may bring challenges to the father-in-law's family after marriage. This is taken seriously in many South Indian and North Indian traditions.

Remedies commonly advised: Consulting a qualified Jyotishi for shanti (pacification) puja, Ketu-related remedies, and checking the exact pada (quarter) of Mula in Yashwant's chart. If born in the 4th pada, the dosha is considered milder. This should be discussed openly with families and a priest before finalizing the match.
